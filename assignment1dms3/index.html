Researching Orb.Farm

Le Viet Minh - s3997682 - Assessment Task 1

  1. It belongs to the simulation sandbox genre, focusing on the elements and components of the ecosystem
  2. Three examples of software in this genre:
    • SimEarth: A simulation of planet's development from microbes to human civilization
    • Sandboxels: A pixel-based sandbox with chemical and physical elements
    • The Powder Toy: A sandbox allow users to interact and experiment with different materials
  3. The program’s interactive mechanics:
    • Arrange element: Combine and arrange natural factors with any order to create a complete ecosystem
    • Control resources limitations: The space is limited so users must balance each different element and think carefully before adding
    • Ecological balance: Simulating a miniature ecosystem needs all conditions like oxygen and carbon dioxide to balance
    • Interactions between elements: Flush according to physical and chemical principles like sand submerged in the water
    • The environment’s impact on organisms: The development of living organisms depends on the environmental conditions
    • Time control: Users can set the time quickly or slowly to see the development of the environment
  4. The program’s mechanics indicate its genre:
    • Arbitrarily arranging and adjusting the elements are the crucial factors that every sandbox application must have
    • Accurately simulate the way an active ecosystem defined for the concept of simulation
  5. The program’s mechanics are novel:
    • Unique graphic style when combining pixels and realistic graphics like water creates an interesting visual experience
    • Simulate the cycle of the ecosystem overtime creating an mini world that is really developing and existing, making users feel its souls
  6. The program’s mechanics are missing:
    • The diversity of organisms: The limitations of the number of biomes so it is impossible to simulate the important factor of the ecosystem like food chains or the relationship between species
    • Weather change: The weather and natural phenomena affect the ecosystem will increase the diversity and authenticity of the system
    • Temperature adjustment: The adjustment of the temperature will open the ability to observe the adaptation of the environment and organisms with different temperatures
    • Interactions between users: Observing and interacting with other users works is really interesting and increasing the uniqueness of the application
  7. This application is mainly aimed at those who are passionate about science, biology and creative thoughts with an friendly interface, focusing on creative and educational experiences
  8. Annoying aspects about interacting with it:
    • The elements sometime move too chaotic will cause a bad control experience when we can not put them in the positions we want
    • The resources are not really diverse, leading to the loss of appeal after we have experienced a time
    • The lack of tutorial is easy to make new users surprised and confused when not understanding how the application works
  9. Enjoyable aspects about interacting with it:
    • The interface and interactions of the application are very simple that we do not have to think too much while using it
    • Full control allows us to be creative with anything we want and not under pressure from achieving any results
    • Observing and enjoying the natural scenery gives us a refreshing and peaceful feeling
