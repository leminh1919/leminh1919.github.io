ABOUT

Neon Light is a vibrant music video bursting with color, energy, and futuristic flair. With retro-inspired aesthetics and a modern twist, it captures the essence of nightlife and self-expression. It’s a bold, immersive celebration of sound, movement, and glowing creativity.

ARTIST

Lila Harper aka Neon Blaze in 1996, is an electro-pop and synthwave artist known for her neon-inspired aesthetic and futuristic sound. With her debut album Electric Dreams (2019), she captivated listeners with catchy synth-pop tracks. Blending 80s nostalgia and modern electro, Neon Blaze’s immersive live shows and fashion collaborations have made her a pioneering figure in the neon music scene.
